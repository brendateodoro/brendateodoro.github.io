Color scheme

The Color Palette for "The Midwest" was inspired by the colors present in the scenario of the Region of the Midwest in Brazil. The color scheme also represents one of the purposes of "The Midwest" website, which is share information about the wheater. The blue color represents the rainy days, as the yellow represents the sunny days.


The header has the color #F2CDA0, which matches with the logo color.


The nav bar contains the colors:#F2CDA0, #F25244, and the #214DA6 for the text.


The h1 have the #214DA6 color and the h2 have #214DA6.


The body of the text will keep the pattern color.
